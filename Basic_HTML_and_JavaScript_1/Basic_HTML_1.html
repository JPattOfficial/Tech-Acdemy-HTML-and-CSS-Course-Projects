Training Siberian Huskies

Training a Siberian Husky puppy involves patience, consistency, and positive reinforcement. Siberian Huskies are intelligent and independent dogs, but they can be stubborn at times. Here are some steps to help you train your Siberian Husky puppy:

Start early: Begin training your puppy as soon as you bring them home, ideally around 8-12 weeks old. Early training helps establish good habits and strengthens the bond between you and your puppy.
